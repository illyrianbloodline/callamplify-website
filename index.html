Why transcript search changes everything

Most businesses record their calls and never listen to them again. The recordings sit in a folder — useful in theory, useless in practice. CallAmplify transcribes every call automatically and indexes the full text, so you can search your entire call history in seconds.

Type any keyword — a competitor's name, a product objection, a pricing question — and every call where that phrase was spoken surfaces instantly, with the timestamp, speaker, source, and AI score attached.
