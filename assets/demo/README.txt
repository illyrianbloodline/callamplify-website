How to make them: ring your own CallAmplify number, book an appointment with
the AI, then download the recording from the call detail page. Calling your own
number means no third-party consent question. Set Settings > Your AI >
caller language to the one you are recording before each call.

Until a file is here the sphere says "Aufnahme folgt in Kuerze" instead of
failing silently. Keep them short (30-60s) and do not add music or edits that
would make the AI sound better than it is - the caption under the section says
these are recorded with our own receptionist.
